Introduction

This year from the 31st August - 8th September, the Verbum Dei Community are organising a volunteering experience with "Comunita Papa Giovanni XXIII" in Reggio Calabria, Italy. Casa dell 'Annunziata of the Papa Giovanni XXIII community in Reggio Calabria works with young refugees from ages 9 to 17 and welcomes them with the aim to help them build their life paths, discover and cultivate interests, and gain qualifications and undertaking social activities whilst they do this.

During our volunteering with the refugee organisation, we wish to undertake a number of workshops that will contribute to their development, both academically and socially. These include educational, physical/sporting and creative workshops that aim to build their self confidence whilst helping them discover their aims in education and later life. Our mission is to grow the confidence of the children and young adults we are volunteering with so they may feel integrated into society and know their self worth in order to achieve their personal and educational goals.
